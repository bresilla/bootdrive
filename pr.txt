Add a mass_storage mode

Hey Dylan! This adds a `mass_storage_mode` so usb-signaller can expose a disk
image to the host, basically letting the phone act as a bootable USB drive.

I'm building a small app (BootDrive) for exactly that, and since usb-signaller
already owns the UDC and the usb_moded interface, adding the mode here made a lot
more sense than running something of my own alongside it and fighting over the
gadget.

The image path comes in through `set_config` (which I wired up), then
`set_mode "mass_storage_mode"` does the rest. Read-only for now.

While testing I also fixed one small thing in `enable()`: the "assume success
when the cable's unplugged" fallback was unreachable because of a `?` on the line
above it, so switching modes failed whenever nothing was plugged in. Happy to
pull that out into its own PR if you'd rather.

Very open to changing any of it, the config format, naming, whatever fits the
project. Thanks for usb-signaller, it's been a pleasure to build on.
